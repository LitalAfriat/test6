vegetaebol omelet

ingredients:

  • Orange lentils 50 gr
  • Flour 10 gr
  • Coneflor 10 gr
  • A bunch of Parsley
  • one onion
  • Brewers' Yeast
  • shawarme spice
  • indian salt
  • water/ vegetarian milk (soya milk, oat milk)


Preparation:

First, mix all the dry ingredients and then add the the water/milk untl youget a uniform batter,similar to egg batter
